About StemLabs

We are a specialized team focused on the full lifecycle of advertising-supported apps: product design, monetization stacks, growth measurement, and long-term ops. We emphasize stability, transparent data practices, and sustainable unit economics—not churn-and-burn titles.

What we build

Portfolio apps designed for scalability across geos and ad ecosystems.

  • Acquisition

    Structured funnels and creative rotations aligned with TikTok, Meta, Google, and other major UA channels—with clear KPIs and continuous iteration.

  • Mediation

    Thoughtful mediation and waterfall configuration to reduce latency, maximize fill across formats, and keep SDK surfaces maintainable across releases.

  • Reliability

    Crash-resilient clients, guarded network layers, and observability so ad-related failures do not degrade core product experience.
