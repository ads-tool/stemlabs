StemLabs use of Google Ads API

This page summarizes how StemLabs (mobile app publisher) uses the Google Ads API in connection with our own advertising accounts. We do not resell API access as a standalone commercial service to unrelated third parties.

Organizational identity

StemLabs is the publishing and integrations brand used in product, dashboards, developer registrations, and partner-facing identity. Supporting legal and organisational context is provided by Binary Bridge Labs. We do not abbreviate Binary Bridge Labs as “BBL”.

Product context

StemLabs builds and distributes consumer apps monetized primarily through in-app advertising (IAA). We buy user acquisition, including campaigns on Google Ads surfaces relevant to mobile growth (such as App campaigns and Performance Max with app-focused goals).

Our internal web application, operational at https://dashboard.stemlabs.site/, aggregates marketing cost with publisher revenue indicators to analyse cost, revenue, profit, and ROAS for our portfolios. OAuth and credential handling are confined to integrations we authorize.

Permitted use (summary)

  • Reporting: server-side ingestion via Google Ads API read paths (e.g., searchStream / GAQL-style reporting) into our Postgres-backed analytics store.
  • Campaign optimisation (planned, governed): limited mutate operations for our own campaigns—budget updates and status changes—with approval workflows, audit logging, and validation where applicable. Broader edits only under the same governance model.
  • Campaign creation (planned): narrow create flows for App campaigns and Performance Max aligned with our UA playbooks, bounded volume, and audit trails—not unrestricted multi-channel campaign builders for unrelated advertisers.
